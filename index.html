HOW WE DO IT

  • C100's membership encompasses over 500 tenured C-suite executives, CEOs, and founders of influential technology companies, whose companies represent tens of trillions in market cap.
  • Our GP-level investor members represent leading investment firms with a combined AUM of over $80 billion.
  • Founders in our membership have built companies collectively valued at over $100 billion.
  • Since its inception in 2010, C100's early-stage fellowship companies have collectively raised over $5 billion in venture funding and created over $15 billion in market value.

OUR PROGRAMS

C100 programs support Canadian founders, CEOs and executives at all stages of their entrepreneurial journey.

Growth

The newly launched C100 Growth Program is for Canada’s most promising scale-stage tech companies. It offers customized mentoring for leadership teams, role-based peer groups, in-person workshops, and access to an extensive network of growth experts, experienced executives, service providers and funders.

Summit

Our annual growth summit event brings together top tech founders and leaders operating worldwide to inspire, educate and connect our highest-performing growth-stage Canadian founders.

Fellows

The C100 Fellows program empowers Canada’s most promising early-stage founders to scale to new heights. The annual fellowship provides high-potential entrepreneurs with unparalleled access to world-class mentorship and a risk-embracing community of like-minded builders.

48Hrs

Our highly-curated tentpole event, “48Hrs in the Valley,” takes place twice a year, bringing our Fellows together in San Francisco to build enduring and meaningful relationships with our network of builders, operators, and investors.
